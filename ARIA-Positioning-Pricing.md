# ARIA — Positioning & Pricing Strategy

## Product Name
**ARIA** — AI Coach for U.S. Insurance Licensing Exams

## Tagline Options
- “The smartest way to pass your insurance licensing exam.”
- “AI that actually understands insurance licensing — not just flashcards.”
- “Personalized coaching for Life, Health, and beyond.”
- “Study smarter. Pass faster.”

**Recommended Primary Tagline:**
> “AI coaching that helps you actually understand insurance licensing exams.”

## Core Positioning

ARIA is an **AI-powered study coach** specifically built for people preparing for U.S. insurance licensing exams (Life, Health, Property & Casualty, etc.).

Unlike generic AI tools or basic quiz apps, ARIA combines:
- Deep domain knowledge of insurance concepts and state regulations
- Structured tools (personalized quizzes, readiness tracking, study plans)
- Adaptive learning based on your weak areas
- Professional, encouraging coaching style

**Target Users:**
- Individual pre-license candidates
- New insurance agents
- Career changers entering the insurance industry
- Agencies training new producers

## Value Proposition

**For Individuals:**
- Stop wasting time on topics you already know
- Get personalized study plans based on your actual weaknesses
- Practice with realistic exam-style questions + detailed explanations
- Track real progress with readiness scoring and streaks
- Feel supported instead of overwhelmed

**For Agencies:**
- Get new agents exam-ready faster
- Reduce failed exam attempts (and re-exam fees)
- Standardize training quality across new hires
- Save trainer time with AI coaching

## Initial Product Scope (Phase 1)

**Focus Lines:**
- Life Insurance
- Accident & Health Insurance

**Geographic Scope:**
- National (all 50 states + D.C.)
- Strong coverage for high-volume states (CA, TX, FL, NY, IL, PA, OH)

**Core Features:**
- Adaptive practice quizzes
- Personalized study schedules
- Readiness tracking + gap analysis
- State-specific regulation lookup
- AI explanations and coaching
- Progress history and streaks

## Pricing & Tiers (Recommended)

### Tier 1: Free
**Price:** $0

**What’s Included:**
- Limited quizzes (5–10 questions per session)
- Basic explanations
- Limited study plan generation
- No progress tracking across sessions

**Goal:** Acquisition + let people experience the quality

---

### Tier 2: Pro (Recommended Main Tier)
**Price Options:**
- **$29/month**
- **$79 for 90 days** (Exam Cycle Pass)

**What’s Included:**
- Unlimited quizzes
- Full personalized study plans
- Advanced readiness tracking + weak area focus
- All state regulations + explanations
- Progress history + streaks
- Priority support
- Exportable study materials

**Best For:** Individual candidates who want to pass efficiently

---

### Tier 3: Agency
**Price:** $99/month (up to 10 seats)  
**Additional seats:** $9/seat/month

**What’s Included:**
- Everything in Pro
- Team dashboard
- Agent progress overview for managers
- Bulk onboarding
- Admin controls
- Usage analytics
- Priority support + onboarding help

**Best For:** Insurance agencies training new agents

---

### Future Tier (Phase 2+)
**Enterprise / White Label**
- Custom branding
- API access
- Dedicated support
- Custom state content
- Integration support

---

## Recommended Go-to-Market Approach

**Phase 1 (Launch):**
- Focus on **Individual Pro tier** ($29/mo or $79/90 days)
- Strong freemium funnel
- Content marketing (exam tips, state guides, “How I passed in 3 weeks” stories)

**Phase 2:**
- Add **Agency tier**
- Reach out to agencies directly
- Offer pilot programs

**Phase 3:**
- Explore white-label / API partnerships with pre-licensing schools
