# ARIA Win-Back Sequence for Cancelled Users

## Overview
This sequence targets users who cancelled their Pro subscription or let their trial expire.

**Goal:** Win them back by reminding them of value, addressing potential objections, and offering a compelling reason to return.

**Tone:** Respectful, appreciative, and low-pressure.

---

## Email 1: Thank You + Soft Feedback (Sent 1 day after cancellation)

**Subject Line:** Thank you for trying ARIA

**Preview Text:** We appreciate you giving us a shot

---

**Email Body:**

Hi [First Name],

We noticed you cancelled your ARIA subscription.

We just wanted to say thank you for giving ARIA a try. We know choosing tools to help with your exam is an important decision, and we’re grateful you trusted us with part of your preparation.

If there’s anything specific that didn’t work for you or anything we could have done better, we’d genuinely appreciate your feedback. Just reply to this email — we read every response.

No hard feelings at all. We’re still rooting for you to pass your exam.

Wishing you the best,

— The ARIA Team

## Recommendations

- **Trigger**: Start sequence 1 day after cancellation or trial expiration.
- **Personalization**: Reference their exam type/state and how far they got if possible.
- **Testing**: The offer in Email 3 is the highest leverage — test different discount levels (30%, 40%, 50%).
- **Compliance**: Make sure all emails include clear unsubscribe links.
