# ARIA Re-Engagement Email Sequence

## Overview
This sequence targets users who signed up but became inactive (no quiz activity, no logins for 7+ days).

**Goal:** Re-engage users by reminding them of value, reducing friction, and offering gentle encouragement.

**Trigger:** No activity for 7 days after last login or quiz.

---
